About AcadVault

AcadVault is a web portal created to digitize certificate handling, add metadata for search, and provide instant QR-based verification.

Objectives

  • Replace manual workflows with a secure portal.
  • Provide role-based access (Admin, Faculty, Student).
  • Generate tamper-evident QR codes for every certificate.

Scope (MVP)

Upload certificates (PDF/Image), metadata capture, search/preview/download, QR verification page, and role-based security.
