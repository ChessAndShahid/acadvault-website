About AcadVault

Project Overview

AcadVault is a zero-budget Firebase solution to issue, store and verify academic certificates using QR codes and role-based access (Admin / Faculty / Student).

Objectives

  • Digitize certificate workflows
  • Provide fast QR verification endpoint
  • Secure storage and role-based controls

Scope (MVP)

  • Guided uploads (PDF/image + metadata)
  • Automatic QR generation & storage
  • Student dashboard (search/preview/download)
  • Public QR verification

Technology Stack

Bootstrap | Firebase (Firestore, Storage, Auth) | qrcode.js | Vanilla JS
